Hello, I'm John Raven M. Latosa from Sorsogon, Philippines. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a focus on Computer Animation at Bicol University, I've explored various art forms since childhood, evolving into a versatile artist skilled in traditional and digital mediums. Specializing in portraiture and painting traditionally, I also engage in 3D modeling, digital painting, and illustrations. Join me in blending technology and art to create a visual tapestry.
