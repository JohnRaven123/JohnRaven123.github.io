WELCOME TO THE CREATIVE UNIVERSE!

Greetings! I am John Raven M. Latosa, a dynamic and passionate individual with a relentless drive for creativity and innovation. Educational Odyssey

Embarking on this odyssey, I pursued a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a specialization in Digital Animation at Bicol University Polangui Campus. My academic sojourn laid the foundation for a comprehensive understanding of computer systems and proficiency in specialized software, including Maya, Blender, and Adobe Photoshop.

Artistry in Motion

Art is not just a skill; it's a language I speak fluently. From painting to cartooning and portrait drawing, my portfolio reflects a mastery of various art forms. My approach to each project is marked by determination and a genuine passion for the craft, evident in the vibrant colors, textures, and light that breathe life into my creations.

Awards and Accolades

The journey has been punctuated by noteworthy achievements, including being crowned the National Champion in the Mixed Art Category at Fun Fiesta sa Pilipinas 2020 and securing the National 2nd Placer title in Editorial Cartooning during the National Campus Press Olympiad 2021. My canvas extends further as I clinched the National Champion title in Likhang Pinta during Fun Fiesta sa Pilipinas 2021.
